## How to Use

Simply type your question in the chat box and press Enter or click Send. The AI tutor will provide detailed explanations and guidance.

- "Explain Newton's second law with an example"
- "How do I solve quadratic equations?"

## Technology

- **Model**: Fine-tuned Llama 3 8B with LoRA adapters
- **Backend**: Unsloth for efficient inference
- **Frontend**: Gradio chat interface
- **Optimization**: 4-bit quantization for memory efficiency
- **GPU**: ZeroGPU for free on-demand GPU access

## Model Information

This chatbot uses a fine-tuned Llama language model trained specifically for educational tutoring. The model has been optimized to provide clear, accurate explanations suitable for high school students.

## Limitations

- Responses are generated by AI and should be verified with textbooks or teachers
- Best suited for high school level content
- May take 5-15 seconds to generate responses depending on server load
- First response after idle period may take longer (cold start)

## Privacy

- No conversation history is stored permanently
- All interactions are processed in real-time
- No personal information is collected

## Technical Details

- **Framework**: Gradio 4.44.0

- **Model Size**: 8B parameters (4-bit quantized)
- **Max Sequence Length**: 2048 tokens
